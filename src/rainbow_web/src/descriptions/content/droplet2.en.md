# What the simulation shows

This visualization accumulates many rays over time. That makes it easy to see how a bright region emerges for the primary and secondary rainbow, while a large portion of the light continues through the droplet.

![Dense outgoing rays indicate directions where the intensity becomes large.](droplet2-overview.svg)

# Physics ideas

When many rays contribute to the same viewing angle, the intensity can be thought of as the sum of their contributions.

$$
I(\theta) \approx \sum_j I_j(\theta)
$$
