# What the simulation shows

This module compares the primary and secondary rainbow. The primary rainbow appears after one internal reflection in the droplet, while the secondary appears after two.

![Primary path on the left and secondary path on the right. Color separation comes from each color having its own refractive index.](droplet-overview.svg)

# Physics ideas

An important quantity is the deviation angle $D$. For the primary rainbow, $D$ has a minimum, and near that minimum many rays leave in almost the same direction.

$$
D_{\text{prim}} = \pi + 2i - 4r
$$
